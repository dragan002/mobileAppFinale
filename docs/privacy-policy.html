Privacy Policy

AtomicMe — Habit Tracker

No data leaves your device

AtomicMe ("the app", "we", "us") is a personal habit-tracking app. This policy explains what information the app stores, how it is used, and your rights over that information.

1. What Data We Collect

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your chosen display name and identity label (e.g. "I am a runner")
  • Habits you create, including their names, descriptions, and the 4-Law fields (cue, craving, response, reward)
  • Daily habit completion records (dates you marked a habit as done)
  • Weekly reflection notes you choose to write
  • Streak history derived from your completion records
  • App settings stored in device local storage (e.g. reminder preferences)

2. How Data Is Stored

All data is stored in a SQLite database on your device. It is never uploaded to any external server, cloud service, or third-party platform. The app does not have its own backend server. The embedded web server runs entirely on your device (127.0.0.1) and is only used to serve the app interface to itself.

3. Third-Party Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transmit any of your data to third parties. There are no analytics libraries, advertising SDKs, crash-reporting services, or external API calls in the app.

4. Permissions

The app may request the following device permissions:

  • Notifications — optional, used only for habit reminders you configure. No notification content is sent to any external server.

No other device permissions (camera, microphone, location, contacts, etc.) are requested.

5. Data Retention

Your data persists on your device until you choose to delete it. You can permanently erase all app data at any time by tapping Profile → Reset Data inside the app, or by uninstalling the app.

6. Children's Privacy

AtomicMe is not directed at children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, the revised version will be published at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
