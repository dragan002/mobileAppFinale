# Privacy Policy for AtomicMe

**Last Updated:** March 30, 2026

## Overview

AtomicMe is a personal habits tracking application that helps you build atomic habits using principles from "Atomic Habits" by James Clear. This privacy policy explains how the app collects, uses, and protects your data.

## Data Collection

### What We Collect

When you use AtomicMe, we collect and store the following information:

- **Identity & Profile:** Your chosen identity (e.g., Student, Athlete, Professional) and your name
- **Habits:** Names, emojis, colors, frequencies, categories, reminders, and setup details (cue location, cue time, routine, reward)
- **Completions:** Dates when you complete each habit
- **Streaks:** Calculated streak counts (days, weekly targets, monthly targets)
- **Reflections:** Weekly reflection notes you write (optional)
- **Achievement History:** Unlocked milestone achievements and dates
- **Preferences:** Notification settings and reminder times

### What We Don't Collect

- Location data
- Contacts or social network information
- Browsing history
- Device identifiers or IDFA
- Third-party analytics or tracking data
- Advertisements or behavioral profiling

## Data Storage

### Local Storage Only

**All data is stored locally on your device.** We do not send your habit data to remote servers. Your personal information is stored in a SQLite database on your phone, protected by your device's native file system security. The app functions entirely offline.

### Database Security

- Habits, completions, and reflections are stored in a SQLite database on your device
- The database is protected by your device's native security (encrypted file system on modern Android devices)
- Data persists until you explicitly delete it

## Data Retention

Your data is retained indefinitely unless you choose to delete it. You can delete all your data at any time using the "Reset Data" option in the app settings.

## User Rights & Data Deletion

### How to Delete Your Data

1. **In-App:** Open AtomicMe → tap your avatar → select "Reset Data" → confirm
2. **Via Email:** If you need additional assistance, contact us at `draganvujic29@gmail.com` and we'll guide you through the process

When you reset your data:
- All habits are deleted
- All habit completions are deleted
- All reflections are deleted
- Your profile is deleted
- The app returns to onboarding state

### Data Export

Currently, AtomicMe does not provide an automated data export feature. To export your data, you can:
- Take screenshots of your stats and habits
- Contact `draganvujic29@gmail.com` for assistance with manual extraction

## Third-Party Services

AtomicMe **does not use any third-party services** for analytics, crash reporting, or advertising. We do not share your data with:
- Google Analytics or Firebase
- Crash reporting services
- Advertising networks
- Social media platforms
- Any third-party data brokers

## Permissions

AtomicMe requests the following device permissions:

| Permission | Purpose | Required |
|---|---|---|
| INTERNET | To sync data with backend services (if enabled) | Optional |
| ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE | To check network connectivity | Optional |
| POST_NOTIFICATIONS | To send habit reminder notifications | Optional |

You can revoke any permission in your device settings, and the app will continue to function (reminders will not be sent if notifications are disabled).

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices. When we make significant changes, we will notify you by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
